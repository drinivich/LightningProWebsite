What is Stem Racing?

Formerly known as F1 in Schools, it is a global STEM competition designed to inspire students aged 9-19 to learn through the creation of miniature Formula 1 cars. Teams of students work collaboratively to design, analyze, manufacture, and test their cars, all while developing skills in various STEM areas, including physics, aerodynamics, design, and manufacturing. The program also involves business and marketing elements.
