The challenge

Formerly known as F1 in Schools, it is a global STEM competition designed to inspire students to learn through the creation of miniature Formula 1 cars. Teams of students work to design, analyze, manufacture, and test their cars, all while developing skills in various areas, including physics, aerodynamics, design, manufacturing, business and marketing.
